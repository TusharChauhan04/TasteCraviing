Our Story

A Journey Served with Love & Syrup

We didn't start in a boardroom. We started with a second-hand waffle iron, a cramped garage, and an obsession with the perfect golden crunch. This is the story of Taste & Craviing - from garage to glory in Ghaziabad.

Our Mission

'Freshly made, made with love, served with happiness'.

Quality

No shortcuts. Only the finest Belgian pearl sugar and local organic milk.

Freshness

Batter made hourly. Waffles pressed to order. Never sitting under a lamp.

Affordability

Street food should be for everyone. Premium taste at people's prices.

Happiness

A warm waffle is a hug you can eat. We serve smiles first, sugar second.

The Craft

Documentary authentic.

The Road to the Street

2021

The Vision

Aakash Chauhan dreams of bringing premium street desserts to Ghaziabad — waffles that taste like Europe but feel like home. The vision of Taste & Craviing starts here, in the heart of Kavi Nagar.

handyman
storefront
2022

First Street Pop-up

Taste & Craviing sets up its first cart near Sector 18, Kavi Nagar. Lines form before we even open. The word spreads — the best waffles in Ghaziabad have arrived.

Today

3+ Urban Hubs

Now serving from three permanent street-side locations across Ghaziabad, maintaining that same garage-born soul and authentic taste.

Aakash Chauhan

Founder & Visionary

"I wanted to create a place where the youth of Ghaziabad could experience premium desserts without worrying about the price. Every waffle we make is a promise — fresh, made with love, and served with a smile."

Aakash remains deeply involved in every aspect of Taste & Craviing, from the quality of ingredients to the experience at each hub. His vision of bringing premium Belgian waffles and mini pancakes to Ghaziabad's streets has turned into a brand the city loves.
