Our Story

A Journey Served with Love & Syrup

We didn't start in a boardroom. We started with a second-hand waffle iron, a cramped garage, and an obsession with the perfect golden crunch.

Our Mission

'Freshly made, made with love, served with happiness'.

Quality

No shortcuts. Only the finest Belgian pearl sugar and local organic milk.

Freshness

Affordability
